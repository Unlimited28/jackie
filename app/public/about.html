About The Royal Ambassadors

Our Mission

To actively challenge and guide all boys and young men to a personal commitment to Jesus Christ, and to equip them to live as His ambassadors everywhere.

Our Vision

To see a generation of young men who are spiritually developed, doctrinally sound, and actively involved in missions and evangelism, reflecting Christ in all aspects of their lives.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Living honestly and uprightly, guided by biblical principles.

Discipline

Cultivating self-control, respect for authority, and a commitment to personal growth.

Service

Demonstrating Christ's love through selfless service to the church and community.

Leadership

Developing the skills and character to lead with courage, wisdom, and humility.
